User Data Deletion

Last updated: July 16, 2026

You may request deletion of data associated with your connected social media and advertising accounts at any time.

1. Revoke Platform Access

First, revoke the application's access in the connected platform's account settings. For Meta, open Facebook Business Integrations, select the application, and remove it.

2. Request Stored Data Deletion

Send a deletion request through our GitHub profile at https://github.com/05tzj. Include the connected platform name and your platform account identifier. Do not include access tokens, passwords, or other credentials.

3. What We Delete

  • Connected account identifiers and stored OAuth authorization data.
  • Publishing and advertising records associated with the requested account.
  • Stored metrics and related operational data, except records we must retain for security or legal compliance.

4. Processing Time

We will verify and process valid requests within 30 days. We will confirm completion through the same contact channel used for the request.
