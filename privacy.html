Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use our web application for managing and publishing short-form video content across social platforms, including TikTok.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information, such as your name, account identifier, and connected platform account IDs.
  • OAuth authorization data, such as access tokens, refresh tokens, scopes, and token expiration times.
  • Video publishing data, such as video titles, captions, descriptions, source URLs, privacy settings, publish status, and platform video IDs.
  • Performance and status data returned by connected platforms, such as views, impressions, clicks, spend, watch-time metrics, and review status.
  • Technical logs and audit records used for security, troubleshooting, and compliance.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to authenticate connected accounts, publish videos when you request it, refresh publishing status, retrieve platform metrics, troubleshoot errors, secure the service, and improve reliability.

3. TikTok Data

If you connect your TikTok account, we use TikTok authorization only to perform actions you request, such as preparing or publishing videos and retrieving video-related account or performance information. We do not sell TikTok user data.

4. Content and Publishing

We process the videos, captions, and publishing settings you provide so that the service can submit them to the selected platform. You control which account and settings are used before publishing.

5. Data Sharing

We share data with connected platforms only as needed to provide the service, such as sending video metadata to TikTok when you publish. We may use infrastructure providers to host, store, or process data, subject to appropriate security safeguards.

6. Data Storage and Security

Access tokens and refresh tokens are stored using encryption when an encryption key is configured. We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect account credentials, publishing records, metrics, and audit logs.

7. Data Retention

We retain connected account records, publishing records, metrics, and audit logs for as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and maintain security. You may request deletion of your data as described below.

8. Your Choices

  • You may revoke TikTok or other platform access from the platform's account settings at any time.
  • You may request deletion of connected account data and stored publishing records.
  • You may choose not to connect a platform account, but publishing and metric features for that platform will not be available.

9. International Processing

Your information may be processed in locations where our service providers operate. We take reasonable steps to protect information in accordance with this policy.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.

11. Contact

If you have questions or requests about this Privacy Policy, contact us through our GitHub profile: https://github.com/05tzj.
